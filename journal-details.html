Gyan Parampara Journal

International Journal of Commerce, Humanities & Social Sciences

1. Aims & Scope

The journal aims to promote high-quality research and academic discussion in Commerce, Humanities, and Social Sciences.

  • Commerce & Management
  • Economics & Business Studies
  • Sociology & Social Work
  • Political Science & Public Administration
  • History, Geography & Anthropology
  • Education & Psychology
  • Language, Literature & Culture Studies
  • Interdisciplinary & Emerging Areas

The journal encourages theoretical, applied research, case studies, and policy-oriented studies.

2. About the Journal

Gyan Parampara is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal committed to disseminating scholarly research globally.

  • Maintains high academic standards
  • Encourages interdisciplinary research
  • Provides global visibility
  • Supports young scholars

3. Author Guidelines

Manuscript Format:

  • Language: English
  • Font: Times New Roman, Size 12
  • Line Spacing: 1.5
  • Word Limit: 3000–8000 words

Structure:

  • Title Page
  • Abstract (150–250 words)
  • Keywords (4–6)
  • Introduction
  • Literature Review
  • Methodology
  • Results & Discussion
  • Conclusion
  • References (APA Style)

Submission Requirements:

  • Original & unpublished work
  • Proper citation
  • Author details separate (blind review)

4. Publication Ethics

  • No plagiarism or data falsification
  • Proper acknowledgment required
  • Confidential review process
  • Conflict of interest must be disclosed

5. Plagiarism Policy

  • Zero tolerance for plagiarism
  • Checked using software
  • Acceptable similarity: 10–15%

6. Copyright Policy

  • Authors retain copyright
  • Journal has publishing rights
  • Open access policy

7. Article Processing Charges

  • Indian Authors: ₹1000 – ₹2000
  • International Authors: $30 – $50
  • Includes peer review, DOI, publication & certificate

No submission charges. Pay after acceptance only.
