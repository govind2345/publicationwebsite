About Journal

Gyan Parampara is a widely indexed, peer reviewed, multidisciplinary international scholarly journal dedicated to promoting research in Commerce, Humanities and Social Sciences.

As an open access journal, published papers receive global visibility and reach scholars, academicians, and researchers worldwide.
